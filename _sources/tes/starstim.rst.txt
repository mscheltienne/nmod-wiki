Neuroelectrics Starstim 8
=========================

The `Starstim 8`_ is a wearable and wireless 8-channel system for targeted TES
stimulation and simultaneous EEG monitoring.

.. note::

    The starstim system also exist in 32 and 64 channels version. Only the 8-channel
    version is available on the neuromodulation platform.

Software
--------

The `Starstim 8`_ connects via Bluetooth to neuroelectronics software, NIC2
(Neuroelectrics Instrument Controller), available on Windows and macOS at this
`download link <https://www.neuroelectrics.com/resources/software>`_.
